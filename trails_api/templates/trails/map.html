🌊 Rivers
Instructions:
  1. Click Load Rivers to display rivers on the map.
  2. Select a river to highlight intersecting trails (shown in red).
  3. Choose Show nearby trails (10km) to display trails within a 10km radius of the selected river.
  4. Click Clear Rivers to remove all river data from the map.
